Fonts:

Lucida handrwiting

Open Sans

The reason why I chose these fonts is that Open sans firstly is a very simple and legible font. It is not hard to read therefore making it accessible to people who might have eyesight problems. I have made most of the headings big in size, and all text is presented in a fairly reasonable size font. Lucida’s handwriting on the other mimics that of cursive handwriting and is also not hard to read either. I added in the Lucida handwriting for the secondary headings and some labelling for images. I added the Lucida font to give a different dynamic to the text on the pages instead of having the same font because this can cause a page to be flat and not aesthetically pleasing. The smallest details can sometimes make the the biggest difference and this added some personality to my page. This essentially is what I am trying to get across to my viewer because this is my portfolio website by me. All text represented on my web pages is vast in colour in comparison to its background this also makes it easier for the viewer to read the text. The viewer of my website should not have a difficult time trying to read and understand the text on the pages because this can put them off and lead them to be disinterested in further engaging with my site.

My web pages make use of a variety of colours but the most dominant of them are dark and light grey, a bit of beige, white, and blush pink here and there. My website is made up of a lot of light cold colours. There really isn’t a bigger scheme of why I chose this pallet but besides the fact that I like them. I have always been fond of neutral colours because I find them aesthetically pleasing and calm. So, I decided to run with this palette because it communicates and reflects my interests and brings my essence of me to life. Apart from me liking the colours they also serve well together and are minimalistic. Minimalism is always the right way to go as it avoids confusion and I tried not to make use of colours that are too contrasting. This can be overbearing for the user and make it difficult to focus and sometimes read text on the page. I also tried to keep in mind people who might suffer from colour blindness and have a hard time seeing certain colours.

Wireframe:

My wireframes remained fairly the same from the start of the process as demonstrated in my wireframes. My wireframes are simple none of the pages are overcrowded and they are minimalistic which like I said before is timeless. Everything the user needs to engage with is clear and because of a user’s previous knowledge from the use of other sites it shouldn’t be difficult to navigate through the pages.
